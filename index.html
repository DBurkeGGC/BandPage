My Top 3 Queen Album Releases:

A Night At The Opera
Absolutely ridiculous, and my introduction to the band known as 'Queen'. With tracks like 'The Prophet's Song' and 'Bohemian Rhapsody', it's no wonder why this album is generally regarded as Queen's finest.
Queen II
Full of many unique and complex pieces, 'Queen II' is sure to have influenced the style of many future metal bands. I had a particular fondness for 'The March of the Black Queen'.
News Of The World
Featuring tracks like 'We Are the Champions' and 'We Will Rock You', this album garnered international fame, eventually attaining multi-platinum status.
